About Project

Problem

Alzheimer's disease is a progressive and irreversible brain disorder that affects millions of people around the world. It is the most common cause of dementia, which is a group of symptoms that affects memory, thinking, and behavior. Alzheimer's disease is characterized by the buildup of beta-amyloid plaques and tau protein tangles in the brain, which lead to the death of brain cells and the eventual loss of cognitive function. Alzheimer's disease is a significant and growing public health concern, especially as the world's population ages. According to the Alzheimer's Association, more than 6 million Americans currently live with Alzheimer's disease, and this number is expected to triple by 2050. The disease not only has a significant impact on individuals and families but also on healthcare systems and society as a whole.

Solution

To overcome the above problem we are building a model which is used for the prevention and early detection of Alzheimer disease. Basically, Alzheimer disease diagnosis depends on the different MRI scan images. Here the person can upload the images of MRI scan and then the image will be sent the trained model. The model analyses the image and detect whether the person is having alzheimer or not. The trained model detects the disease with accuracy. To overcome this problem, we are building the model that detects the disease with high accuracy. Our model classifies
types of Alzheimer disease such as Demented and Nondemented.
